Frequently Asked Questions

Tickets can be purchased from all serviced railway stations and ticket offices in the country, as well as online.

The sale of tickets at the box office stops 5 minutes before the departure of the train. The online sale of tickets is carried out no later than 3 hours before the departure of the train.

From non-served stations where there are no ticket offices, ticket sales are carried out by the transport staff on the train. The price of the ticket in the train from a serviced station is higher than the price of the transport document from the ticket office, according to the Tariff for the carriage of passengers and hand luggage by rail in an internal message.

For international train travel, BDZ customers can obtain information from the International Ticketing and Tourism Offices.

When a passenger reports forgotten luggage at the stations or on the train, the station (train) staff searches for the luggage. Found luggage is handed over against payment of the price corresponding to 1/2 ticket for the kilometer distance. To search the luggage, the passenger pays BGN 2.00. You can report forgotten luggage to the national information telephone number 02/931 11 11.

Amounts for fully or partially unused tickets and subscription cards can be reimbursed by customers according to the specified complaint procedure.

Smoking is prohibited in all categories of passenger trains. The penalty for violating the smoking ban on trains is BGN 20.35.
